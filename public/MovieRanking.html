The Best Movies According to Swastika

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Chhichhore

An ode to college-time friendships, romances, ragging, fights, competitions and countless memories, Chhichhore is a riot of emotions, and takes you on a nostalgic ride.

M.S.Dhoni: The Untold Story

It is really an inspiring movie for all. It teaches us Work hard: Nothing good comes easy.

English Vinglish

It is a a simple yet very effective story of self determination, self respect and love about family and love about ourselves.
